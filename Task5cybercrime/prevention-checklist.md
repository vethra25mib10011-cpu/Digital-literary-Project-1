#  Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

##  Actionable Safety Checklist

  **Use strong and unique passwords**
  - Create passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols  
  - Avoid reusing the same password  

   **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
  - Activate 2FA for email, banking, and social media accounts  

   **Avoid clicking unknown links**
  - Do not open suspicious emails, SMS, or WhatsApp links  

   **Keep your device and apps updated**
  - Install updates regularly to fix security issues  

  **Do not share personal information online**
  - Avoid posting sensitive details like phone number or address  

   **Never share OTP or UPI PIN** 💳
  - No bank or app will ever ask for your OTP or PIN  

  **Do not scan unknown QR codes** 💳
  - Scanning QR codes can result in money being deducted  

  **Report fraud immediately**
  - Use **https://cybercrime.gov.in** or call **1930** for quick action
    
-----------------------------------------

##  UPI & Financial Safety Tips
 **Never share OTP or PIN**
  - Banks and UPI apps never ask for this information  

   **Do not scan unknown QR codes**
  - Scanning a QR code can lead to money being debited  

   **Verify payment requests carefully**
  - Receiving money does NOT require approval or PIN entry  

---

##  Reporting Cybercrime (India)
  **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
  **Helpline Number:** 1930 (24×7)

 **Report immediately if fraud occurs**
  - Faster reporting increases chances of recovering money
